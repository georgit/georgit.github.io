About this site

Wed, 20 Apr 2016

This is my research site! I’ve always put off making one because I figure everyone who wanted to find me could do so already, and I have no idea what to write about, but now that I’m publishing papers, I figure I might as well. Not sure that there will be a lot of posts here, but I am sure there will be no Facebook political opinions, since I think we’re all tired of those. Please also note that as my work is in systems, this site is not going to be flashy or fancy. This is by design.
